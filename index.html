Campaign Buddy

Campaign Buddy is there to help you run any D&D campaign.
Do everything from taking notes, looking up spells/items/character sheets, roll digital dice, and play enviromental background music.
❮ ❯

Campaign Buddy is an Dungeons and Dragons toolbox for Game Masters to use to build and run their games as smoothly as possible by having the information and tools that they need at their fingertips.

Technologies: JavaScript, NodeJS, Less, MySQL, ReactJS, ExpressJS, Jest, BlueprintJS, QuillJS, Enzyme, react-panelgroups, Supertest
